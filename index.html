Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Process

  1. Mix bread crumbs with milk to form dough.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden.
  4. Soak in sugar syrup and serve.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Process

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Cook with sugar syrup and milk to form dough.
  3. Roll and flatten the dough.
  4. Cut into diamond shapes and serve.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Paneer/Chenna Balls
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Process

  1. Curdle milk, make chenna, and shape into balls.
  2. Boil balls in sugar syrup and cool.
  3. Simmer milk with sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  4. Soak balls in rabri, chill, and serve.
